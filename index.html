About This Application
What are Speed Humps?

Speed humps are traffic calming devices designed to slow vehicles down in residential areas and school zones.

Types of Speed Humps:
  • Cushion Humps: Lower profile humps that allow emergency vehicles to pass more easily
  • Permanent Humps: Traditional raised sections across the roadway
How to Use:
  • Use filter buttons to show specific types of speed humps
  • Click markers to see location details
  • Add your location to find nearby speed humps
  • Report new speed humps using the form
  • Get directions between locations
